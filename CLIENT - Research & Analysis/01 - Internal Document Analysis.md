## 7. Strategic Priorities & Initiatives

### "Excellence Unleashed" Strategy (FY2026+)
Three pillars replacing BD2025:

1. **Compete** — Accelerate growth through world-class commercial capabilities; new vertical business unit operating model; Chief Revenue Officer role
2. **Innovate** — Highest-impact pipeline in BD history; biologic drug delivery (GLP-1), connected care, APM, pharmacy robotics, tissue regeneration, urological care, at-home care; AI and robotics
3. **Deliver** — BD Excellence lean system extended to commercial and innovation; $200M cost-out program ($150M run-rate complete as of Q2 FY26); >8% gross productivity improvements

### BD2025 Accomplishments (FY2020–FY2025)
- Added >$5.4B in organic revenue
- Launched 125+ new products
- Expanded adjusted operating margin +560 bps
- 15.1% adjusted EPS CAGR
- CapEx/revenue reduced to 3.5%
- Completed Waters combination (Life Sciences spin-off)

### Waters Combination (Closed February 9, 2026)
BD spun off Biosciences + Diagnostic Solutions, combined with Waters Corp. BD shareholders received ~39.2% of combined entity. BD received ~$4B cash: $2B deployed via ASR + $2B debt paydown. BD now pure-play MedTech.

### Capital Allocation Framework
- Target net leverage: 2.5x (currently 2.9x)
- Dividends: ~$1.196B paid FY2025
- Share repurchases: $2B ASR post-Waters + ongoing
- H1 FY26 shareholder returns: $2.3B ($2.0B buybacks + $0.3B dividends)

## 9. Industry-Specific Considerations

| Metric/Consideration | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Organic / Currency-Neutral Growth** | Primary investor metric; BD targets low-to-mid single digit FXN growth for New BD FY2026 |
| **Purchase Accounting Amortization** | $1.5–2.0B+ annually from C.R. Bard (~$24B, 2017), Edwards APM ($3.9B, 2024), and other acquisitions |
| **Procedure Volumes** | Hospital surgical/interventional procedure volumes drive disposables utilization |
| **Regulatory Milestones** | FDA 510(k)/PMA clearances are key catalysts; BD Alaris 510(k) clearance July 2023 was critical |
| **Capital vs. Consumables Mix** | Consumables (recurring) vs. capital equipment (lumpy); Alaris capital placements vs. infusion set utilization |
| **VoBP in China** | Government-mandated price reductions; headwind to MDS, SM, PI units |
| **BD Excellence** | Lean manufacturing system; >8% annual gross productivity; drives margin expansion |
| **Tariffs** | Active FY2026 risk; BD incorporated tariff policies effective May 6, 2026 into guidance |
| **EU MDR/IVDR** | Compliance costs and transition timeline (extended to 2027–2028 for certain devices) |
| **Ethylene Oxide Sterilization** | EPA compliance deadline extended to April 2028; changes underway at BD facilities |
